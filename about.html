About me

→What I do

I analyse complex business problems and turn them into reliable data systems — from requirements gathering and stakeholder workshops through SQL data modelling, reporting, and development. My core toolkit: SQL Server, C#/.NET, business analysis, CRM systems (Bullhorn, ITRIS, Siebel), financial and payroll processes (bespoke, AccountsIQ, RSM InPay). Currently adding DP-300 (Administering Microsoft Azure SQL Solutions) to extend into cloud-managed SQL.

→How I can help you

If your data is spread across systems and reporting is harder than it should be — I've built management reporting from scratch for finance, operations, and senior leadership. If you're migrating a CRM or replacing a finance system — I've done both, more than once, while keeping the business running. If your technical systems and business processes have drifted apart — I sit comfortably on both sides of that gap, having spent 22 years as the person responsible for all of it.

→Why I can help you

My training is in physics, which means I start from first principles and don't stop until I understand the whole system. Twenty-two years as the sole data and systems lead at a UK not-for-profit recruitment firm — owning requirements, design, build, deployment, support, and training — means no blind spots about what breaks and why. I'm also a lifelong learner in the working sense: I teach at Codebar Brighton, speak at Brighton Data Forum, and the DP-300 work is the current chapter of that.
