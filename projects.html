01Crude Approximate Entropy exploration of a time series

In my last job at a recruitment company the CEO asked me for a tool to help predict changes in payment behaviour of some big clients. The business operates in the non-profit market with many clients in education, and temping being a big part of turnover. Temp work is strongly dependent on holidays with a strong yearly recurrence. I found that Approximate Entropy (ApEn) could be a useful tool.

Tech stack: python, DuckDb
